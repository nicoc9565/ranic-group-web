Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it. If you have questions, email info@ranicgroup.com.

We collect information you choose to submit, such as your name, email address, company name, and the contents of your message. We may also collect basic technical data (for example, browser type and approximate location) through standard website analytics.

If you submit a form, your information is transmitted to our email/processing provider to deliver the message and allow us to respond. Please avoid sending sensitive personal information through the form.
